Phi Beta Kappa Announces Harvard Inductees

by Mary Parker

December 8, 2014

(l to r) Eric B. Zheng, Jonathan A. Marks, Julia M. Y. Pian, Olive W. Tang, Perry S. Choi

Five undergraduates in concentrations administered by MCB have been chosen for the ‘Senior 48’; the Harvard chapter of Phi Beta Kappa. Phi Beta Kappa is the oldest scholastic honors society in the United States, and was founded at the College of William and Mary during the American Revolution. The Harvard and Radcliffe College chapters of Phi Beta Kappa (PBK) joined together in 1995 to form Alpha Iota of Massachusetts.


The MCB seniors include Perry S. Choi from Neurobiology; Julia M. Y. Pian, Jonathan A. Marks, and Eric B. Zheng from Chemical and Physical Biology; and Olive W. Tang from Molecular and Cellular Biology.
