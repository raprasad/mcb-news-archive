Two MCB Postdocs Receive Certificates of Distinction in Teaching

April 15, 2016

(l to r) Matthew Cabeen (MCB 292) and Alexander Mathis (NEUROBIO 105A)

Congratulations to the recipients of the Bok Center’s Certificate of Distinction in Teaching Awards (known as "The Q Awards”).

The award is based on an overall score of 4.5 or higher with a minimum of 5 evaluations on the Committee on Undergraduate Education (CUE) section evaluations.
