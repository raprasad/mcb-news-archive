Out with the Bad in with the Good

May 5, 2009

CLICK HERE FOR THE ACTIVITY POLL!! (poll is over)

As the weather warms up, we all find ourselves resurfacing from winter hibernation.

Not only are we spending more time outside, we're dusting off the lawn mowers, bikes, kayaks, or simply ourselves; all-around spring cleaning has begun.

As you start your summer activities, you might want to consider joining like-minded folks from the MCB community and initiate or participate in one or many activities.

The Department has assembled a list of choices but would welcome more input. Please mark any contest, club, or event that you would consider joining or taking part in.

If there is an activity you'd like to see, simply let us know by emailing Alison Chamberlain at achamberlain@mcb.harvard.edu. We'll start organizing the ones that receive the most votes.
