MCO Student Carolina Salguero Appointed 2016 Horizon Scholar

by Sophie Blum

December 14, 2015

Carolina Salguero, a fifth-year graduate student in Victoria D’Souza’s lab, has earned a prestigious post as one of eight 2016 Horizon Scholars, a cohort selected by Harvard faculty to represent the inspirational achievements of PhD students university-wide.

Salguero will present her project, “To Be or Not to Be a Knot: the Basis of Translational Recoding in Viruses like SARS and HIV,” at the 2016 Harvard Horizons Symposium in April.
