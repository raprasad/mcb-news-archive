Shree Bose (MCB, '16) among Harvard's 15 Most Interesting Seniors

by Sophie Blum

December 11, 2015

This week, the Harvard Crimson profiled undergraduate superstar and MCB concentrator Shree Bose as one of 2016’s “15 Most Interesting Seniors.”

Bose, who won the Google Science Fair grand prize in 2011 and was featured in Glamour magazine last April, is currently working on metabolism neurons in Gary Yellen’s lab at the medical school. Find out what else makes Bose an exceedingly interesting senior here.
