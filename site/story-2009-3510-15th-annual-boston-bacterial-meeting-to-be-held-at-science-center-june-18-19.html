15th Annual Boston Bacterial Meeting to be Held at Science Center June 18-19

by Amy Hitchcock. Camp

June 8, 2009

Boston Bacterial Meeting (BBM) 2009 will take place Thursday June 18 – Friday June 19 at the Harvard University Science Center. The meeting will feature ~25 oral and 60+ poster presentations by students and postdocs representing diverse arenas of bacterial research, including cell and molecular biology, development, pathogenesis, ecology, and evolution.
