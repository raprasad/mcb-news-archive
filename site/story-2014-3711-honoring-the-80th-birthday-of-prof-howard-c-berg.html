Honoring the 80th Birthday of Prof. Howard C. Berg

March 13, 2014

On Friday, March 14, 2014 at 3:00 pm, the Program in Biophysics will host a Visiting Researcher Seminar in honor of the 80th birthday of MCB faculty member Dr. Howard Berg. Presented by Dr. Julius Adler, Emeritus Professor in the Department of Biochemistry and Genetics at University of Wisconsin, the seminar is entitled “The Mechanism of Drosophila Behavior.”

Friday, March 14, 2014
3:00 p.m.
Northwest Building, Room B101 (Basement Level)
52 Oxford Street, Cambridge, MA 02138
Harvard Cambridge Campus
