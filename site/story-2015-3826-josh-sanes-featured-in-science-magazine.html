Josh Sanes Featured in Science Magazine

August 18, 2015

Joshua Sanes contributes optimism (and a great deal of research) to an August 7 Science magazine article on the longstanding controversy of neuronal classification. While skeptics suspect we may never agree on a uniform system of identification for neurons, recent efforts by Sanes and his colleagues, such as the U.S. BRAIN Initiative’s brain-cell census and the drop-seq RNA sequencing technique (published in Cell in May), offer promising advances toward classifying the chaos.
